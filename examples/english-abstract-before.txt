This study aims to examine a critical issue at the intersection of digital transformation and corporate innovation. We believe the findings will have profound implications for the broader landscape of management research.

First, the data shows a clear upward trend. Second, the proposed framework functions as a guide for practitioners. Third, our methodology highlights the importance of cross-disciplinary collaboration.

It is not just an empirical contribution but also a theoretical one, opening new avenues for future research.

Future research should explore how these dynamics unfold across different institutional contexts.
